About

Welcome! I am a researcher at the Technical University Berlin. The field of my interests is related to design and modeling&simulation of mechatronic systems.

Research

Aerospace and Ocean engineering

Systems analysis of complex dynamical systems, design of advanced control systems, optimal control, stochastic, automatic control, model-based design.
